Fitness and lifestyle is a Coaching Company, focused on fitting the training and nutrition to the client and not the other way around. We’ve built our foundation around the fact that every single person we come in contact with is a different individual: different body, goals, hormones, social responsibilities, schedules, dietary history, and views on what “being fit” really means.

Having this understanding, it’s been our role as a team of coaches to assess every aspect of the individual and then develop a plan (both training and nutrition) that allows the most consistency and adherence possible, for that individual.

We stand by our coaching proudly to say that each and every single prescription provided to a client from a FAL coach, will be built for that individual specifically. Never a template or meal plan that locks you into a generic way of eating, but a prescription designed to educate you on what to eat, why you’re eating it, and how it’s creating the result you see.

When it comes to training and nutrition, authentic coaching and education is the key to seeing results that last forever. At Fitness and Lifestyle, it’s our mission to provide world-class coaching, education, and accountability to guarantee our clients achieve the results they came to us for.
